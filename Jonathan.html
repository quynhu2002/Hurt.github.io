A LUCKY SOLDIER

Jonathan Woolvett, a lucky soldier survived World War II, but for him it was a shame that his best friends were killed by the war. This made he feel haunted by what had happened

EVERY TIME I PUT MY HEAD ON MY PILLOW IT'S LIKE A PORTAL TO HELL

    He shared with the Global News website what he had experienced, the memories he had with his friends on the battlefield. These are happy memories mixed with grief

    After that war, he was back home, having a normal life but what he wanted was to become a soldier. Because after that war everything went messy, chaotic, and he does not know how to solve with these problems.
